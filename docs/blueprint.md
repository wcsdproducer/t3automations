# **App Name**: Virtual Receptionist AI

## Core Features:

- 24/7 Availability: Provide round-the-clock virtual receptionist services, ensuring that customer inquiries are always addressed, increasing customer satisfaction and minimizing missed opportunities.
- Answering service: AI-driven call answering to intelligently triage and route calls based on pre-defined rules and caller intent.
- Lead Qualification: Use AI as a tool to evaluate leads based on defined criteria, forwarding qualified leads to sales teams and filtering out unqualified ones.
- Appointment Scheduling: Automate the scheduling process by integrating with calendars and allowing callers to book appointments directly through the AI receptionist.
- Message Taking and Delivery: Record detailed messages and deliver them via email or SMS to the appropriate personnel.
- Customizable Greetings: Enable businesses to create and update custom greetings to align with their brand voice and specific scenarios.
- Analytics Dashboard: Provide a dashboard to track call volumes, lead quality, and other key metrics to evaluate the effectiveness of the virtual receptionist service.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark teal (#31859C) to convey professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F7) for a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Soft orange (#E5954A) for highlighting calls to action and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, providing a modern and neutral look for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean, professional line icons to represent services and features.
- Design a clean and intuitive layout that emphasizes ease of navigation and a user-friendly experience.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to guide users and add a touch of polish.
